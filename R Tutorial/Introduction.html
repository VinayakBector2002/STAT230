This online module introduces you to the software environment R (which is also introduced in Chapter 6 of the course notes). R can be an incredibly useful calculator (eg, for summing up probabilities, computing matrix inverses) and more, which this introduction is offered. MANY of your future courses will use R, so why not get your feet wet now?

The contents are as follows:

Module 1: What is R and how do I install it?

Module 2: Simple operations and variable assignments

Module 3: Discrete probability distributions in R

Module 4: R Tutorial + Exercises (coming soon)

Modules 1, 2 and 3 are offered entirely online through videos and practice questions. Module 4, the R tutorial, will consist of even more practice questions, where we use R to compute STAT 230 probabilities!
